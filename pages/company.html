Founded in 2024 as a Nigerian design & development studio, Syncei has grown into a more than your neighbourhood tech company. Before officially becoming a company in the past 5 years, we’ve assembled a tight-knit team of professionals united by a common aim: to build products that matter.

What we see

A company in the niche of every business, elevating the crafts of average business' to join the changing streams of the world wide web.Technology can be daunting, but we brighten the process by shaking things up and sticking to a positive, healthy approach that helps educate our clients and keeps things on track.

We understand our client partners have a reputation to protect. We don’t “farm out” projects, and we don’t cut corners.
